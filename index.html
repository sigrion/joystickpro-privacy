Privacy Policy — Joystick Pro

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Zintova ("we", "our", or "us") built the Joystick Pro app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Joystick Pro is designed to function as a game controller. The app collects minimal data necessary for its operation:

  • Local network data: Your device's local IP address is used solely to establish a connection between your phone and your PC over your local Wi-Fi network. This data never leaves your local network and is not transmitted to any external server.
  • Camera access: Used exclusively for scanning QR codes to establish a connection with the PC companion app. No images or video are stored, recorded, or transmitted.
  • Device name: A user-configured name (e.g., "Luis's Controller") stored locally on your device and shared only with the connected PC over your local network.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data.
  • We do not maintain user accounts or profiles.
  • We do not store any data on external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: Processes subscription payments. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is not accessible to us. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

4. Subscriptions

Joystick Pro offers an optional premium subscription to remove advertisements. Subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.). You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play Store settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Joystick Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

6. Data Storage

All app settings, custom control layouts, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers operated by us. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.
